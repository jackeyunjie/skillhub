# Gate Criteria

## Advance

Advance when:
- the current critical assumption has directional evidence
- the next investment is proportional to the evidence
- failure at the next step would still leave room to learn

## Explore Next Stage Cheaply

Explore the next stage at low cost when:
- current-stage evidence is promising but incomplete
- later-stage information can be learned without full commitment

This is not permission to scale.

## Continue Current Test

Continue when:
- sample is too small
- signal is mixed but learnable
- the test was biased or unrealistic
- recording fields were incomplete

## Pivot

Pivot when:
- the problem is real but the target segment, offer, channel, or mechanism is wrong
- users show alternative behavior that points to a better hypothesis

## Stop

Stop when:
- the critical assumption is invalidated
- users show no costly behavior
- the next test would be expensive and unlikely to change the decision
