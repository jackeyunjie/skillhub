# Causality Checklist

Ask:

1. Did X happen before Y?
2. Is there a plausible mechanism from X to Y?
3. Did the expected intermediate node move?
4. Are there alternative explanations?
5. Was the sample stable?
6. Was the segment comparable?
7. Could seasonality, channel quality, pricing, or external events explain it?
8. Is there a counterexample?
9. What evidence would disprove the claim?
10. What low-cost test can isolate the driver?

## Confidence Labels

- `correlation-only`: relationship observed but mechanism unproven
- `plausible-driver`: mechanism exists but evidence incomplete
- `actionable-hypothesis`: enough for a limited test
- `causal-enough-for-stage`: enough for current-stage action
- `not-supported`: evidence does not support the claim
